What is this?

HK Screener tracks Binance Futures USDT-M perpetual contracts, cross-referenced with CoinGecko & CoinMarketCap data.

Market Cap Tabs

  • Micro Cap: $25M - $50M
  • Small Cap: $50M - $100M
  • Low Cap: $100M - $250M
  • Mid-Low Cap: $250M - $500M
  • Mid Cap: $500M - $750M
  • Mid-High Cap: $750M - $1B
  • High Cap: $1B - $1.5B
  • Mega Cap: $1.5B+

Columns

  • Symbol Coin ticker
  • Price Current price (USD)
  • MCap Market cap (millions)
  • FDV Fully diluted valuation (millions)
  • 24h Vol 24hr volume on Binance (millions)
  • D1 % 24hr price change
  • 30D % 30-day price change
  • Q4 Low Lowest price Nov-Dec 2025
  • Bounce % bounce from Q4 low
  • Notes Your personal notes (auto-saved)

Color Coding

GREEN = Positive / Gains

RED = Negative / Losses

Filters

  • New Coins Newly added to category
  • D1 Gainers/Losers 24hr movers
  • 30D Gainers/Losers Monthly movers
  • Bounce >100% Big recoveries from Q4 low
  • Has Notes Coins you've annotated

Tips

  • Click column headers to sort
  • Drag column headers to reorder
  • Notes auto-save as you type
  • Use search to find specific coins
