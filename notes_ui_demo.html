Notes UI Options Demo

Option 1: Inline Column

Compact & Quick

A small text input directly in the table row. Best for short, quick notes.

Pros:
  • Always visible
  • Quick to edit
  • No extra clicks needed
Cons:
  • Limited space for text
  • Makes table wider
  • Can look cluttered

Option 2: Expandable Row

Clean & Detailed

Click a row to expand and reveal a larger notes area below. Best for detailed notes.

Pros:
  • Clean table layout
  • Plenty of space for notes
  • Notes hidden until needed
Cons:
  • Requires click to view
  • Can only see one note at a time
  • Row shifting can be jarring

Professional & Persistent

  • Table becomes narrower
  • May need scrolling on mobile

Focused & Minimal

  • Can feel disruptive
