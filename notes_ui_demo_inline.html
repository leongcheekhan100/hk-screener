My Recommendation for 50-100 characters:

Style D (Expandable on Focus) gives you the best of both worlds:

- Compact table layout when browsing
- Full editing space when you need it
- No tooltip complexity
- Works well on all screen sizes

Alternative: Style E (Hover Tooltip) if you prefer seeing full notes without clicking.
